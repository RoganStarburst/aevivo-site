Why we are different

Fewer patients. More care.

Standard medicine runs on volume. You get five minutes, a quick label, and a prescription written before anyone really looked at you. Low energy, stubborn weight, brain fog, broken sleep, these get waved away or band-aided, when they could be understood and resolved.

Aevivo is built the other way around. Your doctor takes fewer patients, and brings in the team, so each person gets real time, real attention, and a plan that genuinely fits. That is what it takes to change the direction of your health, not just quiet its symptoms for a while.

Questions

What people ask before booking.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. The first 15-minute call costs nothing and carries no obligation. It exists so you and the doctor can find out whether this is the right path for you before any money changes hands.

What happens on the call?

You talk. Dr. Andrea listens to your story, your goals, and what you have already tried, then tells you honestly what she would do next in your situation: specific tests, a protocol, or a therapy you may already have in mind.

Do I need lab results before booking?

No. You can book with nothing in hand. If you already have recent bloodwork or reports, the optional questionnaire we send after booking lets you share them, which helps the doctor prepare.

Is everything online?

Yes. Consultations happen by video, so you can join from anywhere. Dr. Andrea consults in Portuguese, Spanish, English, German and Italian.

What does it cost after the free call?

That depends on the path you choose. On the call, Dr. Andrea lays out your options and what each involves, transparently. You decide if and how you continue. Nothing is hidden in the first conversation.

What happens to my data?

Your information is stored securely in the EU under GDPR, and it always belongs to you. We never sell or share it.
