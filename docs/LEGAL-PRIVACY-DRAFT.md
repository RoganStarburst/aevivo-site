## 1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how GENESE SOBRANCEIRA, LDA, trading as Aevivo Longevity ("Aevivo," "we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit aevivolongevity.com, request our Buyer's Decision Guide, submit a reservation, or otherwise interact with us.

Aevivo is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

## 2. What data we collect

We collect personal data in the following ways:

### a. Information you give us directly

When you submit a form on the Site:
- **Buyer's Decision Guide request:** first name, email address.
- **Reserve from Next Batch request:** full name, email address, phone number, role or organisation type, compounds of interest, any notes you add.

When you order or transact with us: the above plus shipping address, tax identification where legally required, and payment identifiers provided via our payment processor.

When you contact us by email or phone: whatever information you choose to share.

### b. Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, we collect limited technical information including IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, and pages viewed. This information is aggregated for site analytics and is not used to identify you personally unless combined with data you submit directly.

### c. Information from third parties

We do not knowingly receive personal data about you from third-party sources for marketing purposes.

Some of our processors, notably MailerLite, may store or process data in countries outside the European Economic Area. Where this happens, the transfer is covered by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards as required by GDPR.

## 6. How long we keep your data

- **Decision Guide leads:** we retain your email until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
- **Reservation and order records:** kept for the duration of the commercial relationship plus any period required by Portuguese tax and commercial law (typically ten years).
- **Marketing consent records:** retained for the life of the consent plus a reasonable period to demonstrate compliance.
- **Support correspondence:** typically retained for up to three years unless deletion is requested earlier.

## 7. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erase** your data, where legal retention requirements do not require us to keep it.
- **Restrict** or **object** to processing, including marketing.
- **Portability** of data you have provided, in a structured electronic format.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@aevivolongevity.com. We will respond within 30 days.
