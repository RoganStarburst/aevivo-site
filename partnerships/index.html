Aevivo Story

Built for what we couldn't find.

Peptides had become one of the most talked-about categories in longevity. The supply chain behind them had become one of the least trustworthy. Origins were vague, documentation was inconsistent, and buyers were asked to trust labels that no one could verify.

Aevivo was founded to build the supply we wanted to buy from: European synthesis, verified purity, and a Certificate of Analysis on record for every peptide. Delivered in a pen format that protects the peptide from production to use.

Suzanna and Tiago met at an art exhibition through a mutual friend. A long conversation about longevity science turned into a shared frustration with how the European peptide market was being supplied: heavy on marketing, light on documentation. Aevivo started as the supplier they wanted to buy from themselves.

Suzanna Howse
Co-Founder

Four decades of building businesses across industries taught Suzanna that the quality of life you lead matters more than what you have in the bank. She came into longevity through her network of forward-thinkers, and an IV protocol experience in Colombia two years ago that she still refers to as the moment it clicked. Her frustration with the current peptide market is the misinformation. Her work at Aevivo is to fix it, one educated buyer at a time.

Tiago Areias
Co-Founder

Tiago spent eleven years in a career that paid well and taught him a lot, then walked away to build something he actually believed in. A year of searching landed him on longevity, and what pulled him in was less the science and more the state of the supply chain, which he saw as broken in ways that were almost embarrassing. His frustration with the European peptide market is plain: too many companies asking buyers to simply trust them, with no documentation, no traceability, no accountability for what is inside the vial. His work at Aevivo is to build the supplier this market has been waiting for.

Support

Frequently asked.

All products sold by Aevivo are intended strictly for in vitro laboratory research and educational purposes. They are not intended for human or animal consumption, clinical use, food additives, cosmetics, or any therapeutic application. Purchasers assume full responsibility for compliance with all applicable laws and regulations in their jurisdiction.
Our peptides are synthesized in European GMP-certified facilities using solid-phase peptide synthesis with Fmoc-protected amino acids. Our lead manufacturing partner is based in the European Union and brings deep, long-standing expertise in regulated peptide synthesis.
Every peptide has a minimum ≥97% release specification, verified by analytical HPLC. Lot release testing consistently exceeds 98%. The specific verified purity for the batch you receive is documented on the Certificate of Analysis, which you can request at any time.
For every peptide in our catalog, we hold a representative Certificate of Analysis on record, including HPLC purity chromatogram, mass spectrometry data confirming molecular weight and sequence identity, physical appearance, and recommended storage conditions. CoAs are not sent automatically but can be requested at any time by emailing info@aevivolongevity.com or via WhatsApp.
Our pre-filled pen system uses sealed cartridges with dial-based dosing. This eliminates reconstitution variables, reduces contamination risk from repeated septum puncture, and enables precise, repeatable dispensing for research protocols.
From close of a batch cycle to delivery at your address typically runs five to fourteen business days, depending on production timing, customs, and carrier. We provide an estimated window at the time of order confirmation.
Peptides ship in sealed pen cartridges under inert atmosphere, with temperature-controlled packaging where required. Storage conditions are specified on each product's Certificate of Analysis and in your order documentation. Most peptides are stable at 2-8°C refrigerated.
EU shipping is our default. International shipping is handled case by case depending on destination regulations and peptide classification. Let us know your destination at the time of order and we will confirm whether we can serve it.
Volume arrangements are available case by case for clinics, laboratories, and pharmacies. Reach out with your context, intended volumes, and timelines, and we will come back with a proposal.
Inspect every package on arrival. Any visible damage, temperature excursion evidence, or documentation discrepancy must be reported within 72 hours of delivery. Confirmed issues are resolved by replacement or refund. All sales are otherwise final once shipped. Full terms are in our Terms & Conditions.
