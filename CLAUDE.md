## Brand Identity

AEVIVO positions itself as a "private longevity laboratory" — think private clinic in Zurich. Discreet, controlled, premium. NOT a supplement brand or generic lab supplier.

### Colors

- Midnight Navy `#0B0F1A` / `#0F1B3D` (primary base)  
- Graphite Charcoal `#1A1F2B` (secondary)  
- Champagne Gold `#D6C5A4` (luxury signal, use sparingly \~4%)  
- Burnt Copper `#A06A4A` (warm accent, \<5%, key moments only)  
- Mineral White `#F5F5F2` (clean contrast)

### Typography

- Headlines: Serif (Playfair Display or Canela)  
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter or Helvetica Neue)

### Language Rules

- All products are "For Research Use Only" (RUO) — never imply human therapeutic use  
- Never say what a compound does to the human body — describe what it has been studied for in published research models  
- No hype, no consumer wellness language  
- Luxury tone: precision, certainty, understatement

## RUO Compliance (CRITICAL)

- Never include assessment forms collecting consumer health data  
- Never use consumer tags like "Gut Health," "Fat Loss" — use mechanism tags like "Cytoprotective," "AMPK pathway"  
- Remove language like "Living your best life" or "boost your performance"  
- Include FDA/EMA disclaimers, buyer qualification, and misuse liability notices
