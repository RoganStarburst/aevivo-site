The Shift

For two decades, longevity meant supplements. Today it means signaling molecules.

The body already speaks in peptides. Insulin, GLP-1, oxytocin, growth hormone-releasing hormone. The language of cellular maintenance is short chains of amino acids, dosed by the picogram, timed to the second.

For most of the last fifty years, that vocabulary lived inside hospitals. The compounds were too unstable, too expensive, or too narrowly studied to leave the clinic. That has changed. GLP-1s rewrote metabolic medicine. BPC-157 rewrote tissue repair. GHK-Cu rewrote dermatology. Epithalon, MOTS-C, SS-31. Each opened a new corridor in the cellular-aging map.

Aevivo exists for a single reason: to make those molecules available the way a private clinic in Zurich would deliver them. Manufactured to pharmacy standard. Independently certified. Documented down to the batch. Discreetly delivered to your door.

We started Aevivo because we couldn't find a peptide source we would trust ourselves to use. So we built one, at the standard a private clinic would build it. Suzanna & Tiago, Co-Founders

Aevivo Story

Built for what we couldn't find.

Peptides had become one of the most talked-about categories in longevity. The supply chain behind them had become one of the least trustworthy. Origins were vague, documentation was inconsistent, and buyers were asked to trust labels that no one could verify.

Aevivo was founded to build the supply we wanted to buy from: European synthesis, verified purity, and a Certificate of Analysis on record for every peptide. Delivered in a pen format that protects the peptide from production to use.

Suzanna and Tiago met at an art exhibition through a mutual friend. A long conversation about longevity science turned into a shared frustration with how the peptide market was being supplied: heavy on marketing, light on documentation. Aevivo started as the supplier they wanted to buy from themselves.

Four decades of building businesses across industries taught Suzanna that the quality of life you lead matters more than what you have in the bank. Considered Questions

What people actually ask before ordering.

Most peptide vendors operate on a grey-market model: lyophilised vials, no compounding pharmacy, COAs that may or may not be current, shipping in foam coolers from Hong Kong or Eastern Europe. Aevivo is the opposite end of that spectrum. Pharmacy compounding, third-party batch testing, EU cold chain, full documentation. The price reflects that, and so does what shows up at your door.
Each peptide carries a different regulatory profile by jurisdiction. Some are FDA- or EMA-approved as named therapeutics; others are research compounds without a therapeutic indication. The Buyer's Decision Guide that ships with every order details the regulatory status of your specific protocol in your country. Buyers are responsible for compliance with their local law.
Pens eliminate three failure points: reconstitution error, dose-measurement error, and sterility error. The peptide is filled, sealed, and dose-locked in the pharmacy. You dial the dose and inject. It is what private clinics do, for the same reason.
Cold-chain courier inside the EU, plain packaging, signature on delivery. Outside the EU we ship case-by-case after a short qualification call. Some compounds and some destinations require additional paperwork.
Aevivo recommends every protocol be discussed with a clinician familiar with peptide research. The Decision Guide is built to be readable by both you and your doctor. We do not replace medical supervision and we do not pretend to.
Public launch on 1 June at standard pricing. Founder's pricing does not extend, does not return, and there is no quiet retroactive discount. We recommend ordering the protocol you intend to run for the first three months before the window closes.
